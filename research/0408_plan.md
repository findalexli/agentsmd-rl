## GLM Learnings

1. **GLM-5.1 quality is excellent** — 98% success rate when not rate-limited. Rewrites are comparable to Opus.
2. **GLM rate limits at ~6 concurrent workers** — use 2-3 workers max, or add backoff/retry.
3. **GLM returns no usage/cost data** — `--max-budget-usd` flag is passed but ignored (Z.AI doesn't meter tokens). Budget cap is effectively infinite.
4. **Cost**: $0 for 143 task rewrites + 8 scouted PRs + 5 scaffolds. Equivalent Opus cost would be ~$1500+.
5. **Failure mode**: GLM fails fast on rate limit (429 within seconds). Real failures are rare (~2%).
